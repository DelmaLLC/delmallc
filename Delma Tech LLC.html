About Us

Delma Tech LLC is a client-centric staffing firm committed to addressing hiring challenges through innovative and tailored workforce solutions. With a strong presence in both the USA and India, we are passionate about driving business transformation by delivering high-quality, cost-effective recruitment strategies. Our approach integrates forward-thinking initiatives and industry insights to stay ahead in the ever-evolving talent landscape, ensuring our clients' success is accelerated. At Cheryl Solutions, we prioritize building lasting partnerships, fostering an environment of collaboration, and cultivating a workforce that is both passionate and engaged. 

Our Mission

Delma Tech LLC promises to deliver cutting-edge workforce solutions by obsessively using technology and automation in our service delivery. By understanding the unique needs of our clients, we provide smart, effective staffing solutions that generate measurable success.

Satisfaction Guaranteed
